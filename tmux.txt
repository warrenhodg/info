Make panes in a window the same
  select-layout even-vertical
  select-layout even-horizontal

Move a pane into an existing window
  join-pane -t <window>

Move a pane into a new window
  break-pane

Copy to clipboard:
  ctrl-b [
  Navigate to start of stuff to copy
  Shift-V to start selection
  navigate to end of stuff to copy
  y to copy
